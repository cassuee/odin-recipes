Dumplings

What are Dumplings?

A dumpling is a small mass of dough that can be boiled, fried, baked, or steamed.

There are a ton of dumpling varieties out there, from pork- and cabbage-filled Chinese dumplings to cheesy Polish dumplings.

Ingredients

  • Mojo Wrapper
  • Meat
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Carrots
  • Leafy Greens
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil

Steps

  1. Cut the garlic, onion, carrots, and leafy greens
    into small bits
  2. Add them to your meat
  3. Add soy sauce and sesame oil
  4. Mix them altogether thoroughly
  5. Portion the fillings and put it in the mojo wrappers
  6. Wrap the mojo wrappers
  7. Use a steamer and steam until they look translucent
